Dixie Wildfire

The Dixie Wildfire incident is a recently viral wildfire going on throughout the Summer. It has burned over “604,489 acres” by August 16th. According to CNN’s article of it, there was an extensive drought made worse by climate change. Which can be a result of the widespread pollution, cigarettes, airplane trails, etc. The wildfire is still burning to this day, it may not be able to be contained but it’s a stepping stone for us to do better.

The Camp Fire

The Camp Fire of 2018 is one of the deadliest and devastating wildfires in california as it killed various civilians and burned countless structres down

Wildfires are very devastating as they destroy our wildlife and our cities as they spread very quickly and destroy anything in its path. Wildfires can also have a horrible secondary effect as they pollute our air and the poluted air can quickly spread as seen with some california wildfires that have brought polluted air to Newyork.
